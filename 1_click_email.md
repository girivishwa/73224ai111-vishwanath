# Schedule Trigger
- Sign in to n8n
- Click “Create Workflow.”
- Click “Add First Step.”
- Search for “Schedule Trigger.”
- Configure the schedule according to your preferences.
- Click “Execute Step.”
- Click the “+” button to add another node.
- Search for “Gmail.”
- Select “Send Message.”
- Sign in with your Gmail account to authenticate.
- Enter the recipient’s email address.
- Enter the subject and write the email message you want to send.
- Click “Execute.”
# Chat Trigger
- Click “Create Workflow.”
- Click “Add First Step.”
- Search for “Chat Trigger” and select it.
- Click the “+” button to add another node.
- Search for “Gmail.”
- Select “Send a Message.”
- Enter your Gmail account (authenticate if needed).
- Enter the receiver’s Gmail address.
- Enter the subject and message you want to send.
- Click “Execute Workflow.”
